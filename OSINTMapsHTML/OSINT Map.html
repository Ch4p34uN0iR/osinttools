Tab Descriptions
Goals/Scoping
The initial tab is for defining your engagement.
OSINT Process
Modelled after OSINT Process (Yoga) The process tab will help guide you through your data collection.
OSINT Data Collection
The data collection tab is where your data you harvest would be entered (results from searches, screen shots and any other interesting data).
Additional Resources
The last tab has additional resources to round out your data collections(Ex: additional areas to search, tools and collection of OSINT sites).
